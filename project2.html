nextdoor design

Through the Kleiner Perkins Program, I interned at Nextdoor this summer, a mission-driven startup headquartered in SF. Nextdoor is a private social network oriented towards helping people connect online with their communities and neighbors. During my time at nextdoor, I primarily worked on the Triggers+Engagement team, as well as the Sponsored Posts team. On the side, I helped out with communications and marketing.

As part of the Engagement team, I helped with the flow and interaction design of New User Exeperience and Regular UX surrounding push notifications for iOS. In re-thinking how we can build trust for our users and help increase Nextdoorâ€™s engagement, my project led to a 30% increase in daily users with push notifications turned on.

The second half of my summer was spent re-designing advertiser UX, as well as completely re-hauling an internal ad tool. Please email me for more details regarding my internship!

kleiner perkins fellowship

This summer, I was a part of the KP Fellowship, a program created by the VC firm Kleiner Perkins to foster new relations with college students through internships with their portfolio companies. The fellowship is a supplement to the internship, with around 10 official events scattered throughout the summer, which included talks with KP Chair John Doerr, CEO's of Nuna Health and Slack, and various diversity and networking events.
