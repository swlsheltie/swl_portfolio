paint the pavement

overview

Paint the Pavement is a national program that encourages community building, increases and promotes public safety, and uses public art as a means to transform dangerous roadways and interactions into neighborhood assets.

For this project, we were given 8 weeks build a visual brand and identity for this initiative. Using expressive typography as our main tool with the support of color, texture, and shape—but no pictorial image—I created a poster, overview and application packet, bookmarks, folders, mailers, and accompanying website.
